Tomato Sauce

Description

This delicious tomato sauce with peas suits many different dishes.

Traditionally, it's cooked with pasta. I most often use it with pumpkin filled Tortellini.

The peas make a great source of plant-based protein! Great for vegetarian diets.

This recipe makes sauce for two servings.

Ingredients

  • Half an onion;
  • Two cloves of garlic;
  • Olive Oil;
  • Two tomato sauce cans (200g each);
  • Approximately 200g of frozen peas;
  • Salt;
  • Pepper;
  • Smoked Paprika;
  • Oregano;

How to Prepare

  1. Chop the onion and the Garlic in very small pieces.
  2. In a small pan, cook the frozen peas in boiling water for about 20 minutes.
  3. In a separate bigger pan, pour a bit of olive oil in and light the stove on about 200°C.
  4. Put the chopped onions in the bigger pan and let them sauté for a few minutes.
  5. When the onion is transparent, add the garlic to the pan too and let them sauté for a minute.
  6. Put the tomato sauce and the seasoning in the pan and mix it until it boils.
  7. When the peas are ready, drain the water and mix them with the sauce.
  8. Let it cook for about 5 minutes.
  9. Enjoy it with your favorite pasta.
